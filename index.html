About Me

Hello, my name is Aaron Davis and welcome to my portfolio.

My journey to becoming the Digital Media Expert and Full Stack Developer I am now has been unorthodox to say the least, but an advantageous experience I would welcome all over again.

My professional career began in the financial industry. Working for companies such as Fidelity and TD Ameritrade, I had my sights on becoming a trader and active player in the stock market. During this time I was able to acquire a great foundation of skills such as Team Building, People Management, and Client and Account Management. Unfortunately, due to the downturn of the economy at the time and the volativity of the industry as a whole I began to realize that playing the stocks was not going to be something that interested me in the long term.
